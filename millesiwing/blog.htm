Bordeaux Primeur - The title sous

Have you ever heard about the Bordeaux Primeurs sale?

This event occurs every year and is an important moment in the annual wine business calendar for Bordeaux wines.
The Primeurs sale is the commercialisation of Bordeaux > Grands Crus >> wines, five or six months after the time of the last harvest.
The wines bought are then kept in wineries for ageing until bottling two or three years later.
This event enables consumers to buy these wines at lower rates than when the bottles are released later on the market. The producers take also
advantage of these sales to increase their cash flow.

However, there has always been much controversy regarding these sales, as the wines tasted at the Primeurs sales are ⩽ unfinished >> wines. Notations
and comments given by wine experts are kinds of bets on the future.

Don't worry, we are not serving ⩽ unfinished >> wines during a wine tasting in Paris ! Especially for Bordeaux wines, we like them with a few years on the label..

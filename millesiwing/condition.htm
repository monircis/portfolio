Article 2: The services

  • The wine tastings organized by the Company in restaurants and other places.

Article 3: Booking and Payment

Depending on the services, the Company offers different payment options.
Wine dinners and Wine tastings offered within the Company website are payable online unless specified.
Full payment is required to validate the reservation using Paypal (Visa and Mastercard cards accepted with Paypal).
Once a reservation is completed after full payment, a voucher is issued by the Company to the Customer.
The Customer is to provide a voucher at the beginning of the service.
The Company does not charge a service fee for processing credit card payments.
Services other than Wine dinners and Wine tastings offered within the Company website, and ordered on booking, may be subject to means of payment
other than online payment.

Article 4: Cancellation

Cancelling a reservation may result in cancellation fees being applied. These fees will be charged as follow:

  • 72 hours before the service begins, 10% of the total price order, except for Grands Crus tasting where 25% of the total price will be charged.
  • For cancellations less than 72 hours before the service begins, 100% of the total price order.

Article 5: Interruption

Any interruption during the service, due to the Customer's own, will not allow any reimbursement. The Company may interrupt a service in case of abnormal behavior to at least one Customer. Such a cancellation will not allow any reimbursement.

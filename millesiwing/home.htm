What we can offer

ooking for an alternative to tan ordinary evening or dinner in Paris? Why not spend some time with us to sample different French wines with the explanation of a French sommelier during a Private Wine Tasting?

In beautiful and historical Parisian venues, our wine tours will introduced you to the different French wine regions, wine styles and grape varieties.

Our wine events in Paris are designed for small private groups - minimum 2 persons - and are offered in English. However, they can be offered in Spanish, Russian and Mandarin, on request.

Among the wine tastings experiences we offer. "France in the bottle" wine tour will introduce you to the main French wine regions and styles. We will bring you to Champagne, Burgundy, the Loire valley, Bordeaux and two other French regions.This wine event is available every day. Just contact us and give us your appropriate date! "Grands Crus" wine tasting is a unique wine experience with a selection of several premium French wines, to immerse yourself in the best of the French art de vivre!

We also offer wine events along with a full dinner for wine and food pairing experiences! You can check out our wine dinner tours : "Parisian bistrot" and "Paris Normandy" wine tasting dinners.

"Parisian bistrot" dinner tour occurs in a small and friendly bistrot for a typical Parisian wine experience with delicious food.

"Paris Normandy" wine dinner occurs in a historical, traditional and beautiful Parisian restaurant, where you will immerse yourself in the ambiance and in the unique way of living of the early 1900's in Paris!

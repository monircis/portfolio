The team

A team of passionate sommeliers for your wine tasting

Alexis caraux - Founder of Millesiwine

Millesiwine - combination of French word millesime (vintage) and wine, is created in 2012 by Alexis Caraux to share his passion of wine.

Alexis comes from a strong family tradition of restaurant ownership. He entered the wine business in 2011, after earning the Advanced Diploma of the Wine Spirit Education Trust program.

In 2011, Alexis started organizing wine tastings in French, soon followed by classes in English and Chinese!

Today, Alexis has brought on board passionate people with strong knowledge of both wine and tourism, as well as extensive experience in the two sectors, to make each wine tasting a unique moment.

Whether you are a professional in tourism or other businesses, we can organize a private wine tasting or a private wine dinner in one of our restaurants, or in any other place you might suggest !

Let us help you organize a great time for you or for your clients!
